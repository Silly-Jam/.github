# Silly Jam Pte. Ltd. — makers of vynr

Silly Jam Pte. Ltd. is a Singapore software company building thoughtful, characterful products for people who care about the things they collect, remember, and enjoy.

Our first product is vynr — a modern wine cellar, atlas, journal and ageing companion for wine enthusiasts and collectors.

vynr

vynr helps collectors understand what they own, what they have tasted, what is ready to drink, and why.

It combines:

* Native app design
* Wine reference data
* Maturity and ageing intelligence
* Atlas-style regional context
* Tasting notes and memories
* AI-assisted enrichment with provenance
